The GOV.UK proposition is to help people in departments and agencies who need to publish information or services for the public to use.

Document

GOV.UK proposition

HTML

Details

The proposition helps people to decide if part or all of their planned publishing should be on GOV.UK or not.

It explains:

  • the criteria for content published on GOV.UK, for example putting the user’s needs first
  • what doesn’t go on GOV.UK, for example general advice
